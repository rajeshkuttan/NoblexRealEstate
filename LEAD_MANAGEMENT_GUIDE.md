# Lead Management Guide - UAE Real Estate

## 🎯 How to Create Leads

### Method 1: Quick Lead Creation
1. **Click "Add Lead" button** in the top-right corner
2. **Fill Basic Information**:
   - Full Name (required)
   - Email Address (required)
   - Phone Number (required)
   - Company (optional)
   - Position (optional)

### Method 2: Comprehensive Lead Creation
1. **Navigate to Leads page**
2. **Click "Add Lead" button**
3. **Complete all tabs**:
   - **Basic Info**: Contact details and lead classification
   - **UAE Details**: Emirates ID, Visa Status, Nationality, Business Info
   - **Preferences**: Property type, location, area, budget
   - **Requirements**: Specific needs and document requirements
   - **Assignment**: Team member assignment and lead scoring
   - **Additional**: Notes, tags, and compliance status

### Method 3: Import Leads
1. **Bulk Import**: Upload CSV/Excel file with lead data
2. **API Integration**: Connect with website forms, CRM systems
3. **WhatsApp Integration**: Import from WhatsApp Business

## 📈 Lead Management Best Practices

### **1. Lead Creation Process**
```
Website Inquiry → Lead Form → Qualification → Assignment → Pipeline
```

### **2. UAE-Specific Requirements**
- **Emirates ID**: Required for all UAE residents
- **Visa Status**: Resident, Tourist, Investor, Student
- **Trade License**: For business leads
- **Bank Statement**: 3 months for financial verification
- **Salary Certificate**: Employment verification

### **3. Lead Scoring Criteria**
- **Contact Quality** (25 points): Complete info, valid contacts
- **UAE Compliance** (20 points): Emirates ID, visa status
- **Financial Capacity** (25 points): Budget, salary certificate
- **Property Match** (15 points): Clear requirements, timeline
- **Engagement Level** (15 points): Response time, interactions

### **4. Communication Strategy**
- **WhatsApp**: Primary communication channel in UAE
- **Email**: Formal communications, documents
- **Phone**: Urgent matters, negotiations
- **Video Calls**: Property tours, meetings

### **5. Follow-up Schedule**
- **Hot Leads**: Daily follow-up
- **Warm Leads**: Every 2-3 days
- **Cold Leads**: Weekly nurturing
- **Lost Leads**: Monthly re-engagement
